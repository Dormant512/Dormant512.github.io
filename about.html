Quartersketch

Once upon a time I wanted to one-up my drawing skills by drawing quick sketches daily. Me and a couple of my friends set a tradition of gathering in discord and drawing 15-minute sketches on topics that we made up on the go. I called it Quartersketches (quarter of an hour per sketch). The golden rules of quartersketches are:
  1. Not shaming anybody's drawing skills and not shaming anyone in general.
  2. One participant names a broad topic. For example, "medieval buildings with a modern twist".
  3. Participants request each other specific sketches. For example, a requested sketch on the topic above could be "a round watchtower with an external elevator".
  4. All participants draw requested sketches in just 15 minutes. By the way, I am planning to write a timer discord bot optimised for quartersketches.
  5. Repeat.
